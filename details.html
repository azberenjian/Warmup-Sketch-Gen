Blind Warmup Sketch Prompt Generator

For myself and other creative minds, we often find ourselves stuck in art block; the inability to produce fresh and new creations, or sometimes any creations at all.

With the blind prompt generator, artists are presented with ideas to make quick sketches from an extensive list. It serves as a great tool to break art block, or just as a simple tool for warm up sketches before working on bigger projects.

Enjoy!
